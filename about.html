About Us

Tinka's Foodhub was founded with the vision of bringing diverse culinary experiences to our community.If you're looking for somewhere to eat out in Entebbe, we feature flavours from around the world and options to suit every budget. Whether you are looking for a coffee break or a more leisurely experience, we have something for everyone, from golden-fried chicken, crispy Italian pizzas to delisious ice-cream.

Our Values

We believe in quality, community, and sustainability. Our ingredients are sourced locally whenever possible, and we strive to create a positive impact in our community.
